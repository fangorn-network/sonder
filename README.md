For listeners, we offer the ability to monetize your taste instead of paying to have it harvested.
Everything you do, from play to skip, is a value creation event. 
This is the opposite of today's prevailing gamblification blitz that attempts to commodtize every delta. 

For artists, we offer various data products built on aggregate user signals. You pay directly for data.
1. Per-Track Transition Readout - beore you rtrack, listers were --- over there ---. After they listened, they went --- off yonder ---. User who skipped when to --- a strange place ---.
2. Per artist Peer Radar: which artsists/songs do people who listen to me ALSO like?
3. Per artist neighborhood density map: where do your fans go when they're not listening to you? map of adjacent taste territory
4. Per Track session context report: what kinds of 'sessions' does this track appear in? long sessions? high velocity? late nigh listening? the 100th track in a playlist?
5. Per Artist Retention: given someone listened to my song, how many came and listened to *more* of my songs? a churn metric.
   1. this is NOT followers - that's a platform fabrication.
